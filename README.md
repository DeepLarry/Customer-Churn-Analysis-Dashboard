This project focuses on analyzing customer attrition in an online retail setting. With a 53% Churn Rate, 
the goal was to dig deep into the data, identify why customers are leaving,
and pinpoint high-risk segments to help the business take immediate action.

**Key Features & Functionality**
Interactive Analytics: Powered by Excel Slicers for real-time filtering by promotion response and satisfaction levels.

High-Risk Identification:    A dedicated "Alert System" that identifies 20 high-risk customers based on low satisfaction and short tenure.
Churn Driver Analysis   :    Visualized the relationship between satisfaction scores, support contacts, and promotion effectiveness.
Tenure Trends           :    A line chart depicting how customer loyalty changes over the years.

**Key Insights (What the Data Says)**
The Churn Reality: Over half **the customer base (53%) has left**, despite satisfaction scores being relatively stable (~2.7–2.8).

Immediate Risk:  Identified 20 specific customers who are new (<1 year) and highly unsatisfied (Score <2), requiring immediate retention efforts.
Promotion Gap :  Analysis shows that customers who "Ignored" or "Unsubscribed" from promotions correlate with higher churn.

**Tools Used**
Microsoft Excel: Advanced Pivot Tables, Slicers, and Data Modeling.

Data Visualization: Custom 3D Bar Charts, Doughnut Charts (Gauge style), and Dynamic Line Trends.

Business Intelligence: Created Actionable Insight cards for executive decision-making.
